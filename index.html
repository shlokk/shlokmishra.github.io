Shlok Mishra

I am a Ph.D. student at University of Maryland in the department of Computer Science. I am advised by Prof. David Jacobs working on problems in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I also obtained an MS in Computer Science from University of Maryland, College Park.

I primarily work in Self-Supervised Learning and Generative models in Computer Vision. I have done multiple internships at Google. In Summer of 2022, I worked with Dilip Krishnan on Self-Supervised learning. In Summer 2021 I worked with Christian Haene and Hossam Isack on Generative models. In Summer 2020, I worked with Kuntal Sengupta, Vincent Chu and Sofien Bouaziz on Spoof Detection. I have also done 4 student research internships and have been working in Google for last 2.5 years.
